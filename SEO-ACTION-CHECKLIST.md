## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: When will I see results?**
A: First indexing: 1-2 weeks. Ranking improvements: 2-6 months.

**Q: Do I need paid advertising?**
A: No! Focus on organic SEO first. Paid ads can complement later.

**Q: How often should I update content?**
A: Minimum: Monthly. Ideal: Weekly blog posts + project updates.

**Q: Should I focus on local SEO?**
A: If targeting local clients, yes! Add LocalBusiness schema and Google Business Profile.

**Q: What's most important?**
A: Quality content + technical SEO + backlinks. In that order.
